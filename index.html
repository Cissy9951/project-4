About Me

My name is Qian Xi Luo, and I go by Cissy. I was borned in China, raised in Canada, and earned my BFA in Industrial Design from Rhode Island School of Design in the US.

I enjoy sewing and trying new things, including new snacks, new recipes, or jumping into a project that needs some kind of new skill. By constantly engaging with what I don't know, I get to discover the commonalities and connections between objects, ideas, and events. I benefit from observing how and why certain things are delightful. Then, when I encounter an undelightful situation, I can identify whatâ€™s creating the discomfort more effectively, and find a better solution.

Recycling, reusing, and reducing are very important to me too, and I incorporate these principles of sustainability into my work through designing long lasting, versatile, and disassemblable products.
