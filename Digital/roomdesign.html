Hotel Room Design Game

This game does not work for touch screen devices.

Click Here or Images to Enter Game.

Make combinations of bed, shelf, and seating to attract different clients! Remember to release mouse whenever furniture is dragged into the room region.

This game is made as an exercise to build game experiences using web coding languages, so to make a very easily accessible and convenient game. Isometric perspective is used to invoke dimensionality without stressing over perspectives, and players would be able to place furniture anywhere they want without mismatched perspectives. The furniture are designed in no particular style, so that the correct answers are not obvious. Players would spend more time exploring combinations, and the game does not try to direct players in their room decorating process.
