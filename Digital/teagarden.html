Tea Garden

Tea Garden is a carefully designed chain reaction of silent movements that gives a novel experience to tea tasting. Nitinol, an alloys of nickel and titanium, is the key material that enables the design. This alloys may be deformed (stretched) when it is cool, and will return to its remembered (original) state or length when heated.
